Hi, I am a research scientist with 13 years of experience. For the last 11 years, I studied the Sun at Stanford University. I predicted space weather by analyzing millions of images of the Sun, taken by the NASA Solar Dynamics Observatory, with machine learning algorithms. I also authored a book titled Machine Learning, Statistics, and Data Mining for Heliophysics.

I previously worked at the Harvard-Smithsonian Center for Astrophysics on NASA missions called TRACE and Hinode.

I am committed to the open source scientific software community as Vice Chair of the Advisory Board for the SunPy Project and Data Science Editor for the Journal of Open Source Software.
